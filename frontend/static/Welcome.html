About Us

Welcome to DonorPulse — your trusted platform for smart, efficient, and secure blood donation management. Our mission is to revolutionize the way blood donation is handled by integrating technology with care.

At DonorPulse, we ensure a seamless experience for both donors and administrators through our advanced system featuring Aadhar-based donor identification, eligibility automation, and machine learning–powered blood volume predictions.

We believe in supporting every donor before, during, and after donation with features like automated token generation, donation interval tracking, personalized diet recommendations, and full donor history records. Join us in making blood donation smarter, safer, and more impactful.
